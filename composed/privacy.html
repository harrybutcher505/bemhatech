Composed

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 June 2026

1. Overview

Composed is a desktop application. Your CV, cover letters, job data, API keys, and application history are stored locally on your own machine and are never transmitted to us. We have no access to this data.

2. Data we collect

We collect minimal data:

  • Purchase data: When you buy Composed Pro via Paddle, Paddle processes your payment and shares your email address and name with us solely to deliver your licence key. We do not store payment card details.
  • Support emails: If you contact us by email, we retain that correspondence.
  • Website analytics: Our website may collect anonymised usage data (page views) via Netlify's built-in analytics. No personal data is collected.

3. Data we do NOT collect

  • Your CV or resume
  • Your cover letters or application history
  • Your Anthropic API key
  • Any data entered into the Composed app

4. Third-party services

  • Paddle: Handles payment processing. Subject to Paddle's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic: Processes AI requests using your own API key. Subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

5. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU, you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you (limited to purchase and support data). Contact us at harrybutcher@hotmail.com to exercise these rights.

6. Data retention

Purchase records are retained for as long as required by Dutch tax law (7 years). Support correspondence is retained for 2 years.

7. Contact

Data controller: Harry Butcher, Netherlands. Email: harrybutcher@hotmail.com
